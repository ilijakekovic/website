Interstellar

Interstellar is a thought-provoking science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan, which follows a group of astronauts led by Cooper (played by Matthew McConaughey) as they embark on an intergalactic voyage through a wormhole in search of a new habitable planet for humanity's survival, exploring complex themes of time, space, and the human spirit along the way.

Director: Christopher Nolan
